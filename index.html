Jimut Bahan Pal


I am currently pursuing M.Sc. in Computer Science from Ramakrishna Mission Vivekananda Educational and Research Institute. I am a former student of St. Xavier’s College, Kolkata, where I pursued B.Sc. in Computer Science from the year 2016-2019. I am broadly interested in Deep Learning and Computer Vision.

I am fortunate to have been advised and mentored by fantastic researchers and engineers during the course of my undergraduate and postgraduate studies. In the past I have worked under the supervision of Prof. Shalabh Agarwal (former HOD of CS, St. Xavier’s College). I have built a preference-based location finder application named Wisp for my B.Sc. dissertation. In the postgraduate I have been mentored by Tamal Maharaj and Dripta Maharaj and did several projects in Computer Vision and Deep Learning under their supervision.
