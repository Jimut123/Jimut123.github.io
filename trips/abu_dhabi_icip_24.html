Morocco and Qatar Trip - Oct, 2024

The International Conference on Medical Image Computing and Computer-Assisted Intervention (MICCAI) 2024 was held in the vibrant city of Marrakesh, Morocco, from October 6th to October 10th. I had the privilege of attending this prestigious event to present my poster titled Convex Segments for Convex Objects Using DNN Boundary Tracing and Graduated Optimization. The PDF of the paper can be accessed here: MICCAI 2024 Paper.
View from Hotel in Abu Dhabi

Arrival in Abu Dhabi

The journey to Abu Dhabi for ICIP 2024 began with this stunning view from my hotel room. The cityscape offered a perfect introduction to this modern metropolis, setting the stage for both the conference and exploration.

ADNEC Conference Center - ICIP 2024 Venue

Conference Venue

The ICIP 2024 conference was hosted at the Abu Dhabi National Exhibition Centre (ADNEC), a world-class venue with excellent facilities for international academic gatherings. The modern architecture provided the perfect setting for knowledge exchange and networking.

My name written in Arabic

Cultural Experience

One of the highlights of visiting Abu Dhabi was experiencing the local culture. Having my name written in beautiful Arabic calligraphy was a special souvenir to commemorate the trip.

Premier Inn illuminated at night

Evening in Abu Dhabi

The Premier Inn, beautifully lit at night, served as comfortable accommodation during the conference. Abu Dhabi comes alive after sunset with spectacular lighting that highlights the city's modern architecture.

The iconic Etihad Towers

Etihad Towers

The Etihad Towers are among Abu Dhabi's most recognizable landmarks, representing the city's rapid development and architectural prowess. These magnificent skyscrapers have become symbols of modern Abu Dhabi.

Another perspective of the Etihad Towers

Architectural Marvels

This different angle of the Etihad Towers showcases their impressive design. The complex consists of five towers of varying heights, creating a distinctive silhouette against the Abu Dhabi skyline.

Louvre Abu Dhabi Museum

Cultural Heritage

The Louvre Abu Dhabi is a remarkable cultural institution that bridges Eastern and Western art and civilization. Its iconic dome creates a "rain of light" effect, symbolizing the diffused light that filters through palm fronds in an oasis.

Julius Caesar exhibit at Louvre Abu Dhabi

Historical Treasures

The Louvre Abu Dhabi houses an impressive collection of historical artifacts, including this Julius Caesar exhibit. The museum offers visitors a journey through human creativity spanning thousands of years.

Ancient Egyptian mummy at Louvre Abu Dhabi

Ancient Egypt

This well-preserved mummy exemplifies the ancient Egyptian funerary practices and beliefs in the afterlife. The Louvre Abu Dhabi's collection spans various civilizations, offering insights into humanity's shared history.

Classical statue at Louvre Abu Dhabi

Classical Art

This beautiful classical statue represents the artistic achievements of ancient civilizations. The Louvre Abu Dhabi carefully curates works that highlight the interconnectedness of human artistic expression across different cultures.

Nataraj statue at Louvre Abu Dhabi

Eastern Art

The Nataraj, depicting Lord Shiva as the cosmic dancer, represents the cyclical nature of creation and destruction. This masterpiece showcases the museum's commitment to representing diverse cultural and religious traditions.

Area near Emirates Mall

Urban Exploration

The area surrounding Emirates Mall offers a glimpse into Abu Dhabi's modern urban planning and retail landscape. The city combines cutting-edge architecture with thoughtful design to create inviting public spaces.

Another perspective near Emirates Mall

Modern Architecture

This view showcases the blend of contemporary design and functionality that characterizes Abu Dhabi's newer developments. The city continuously evolves, adding impressive structures to its already remarkable skyline.

The luxurious Emirates Palace

Luxury Defined

The Emirates Palace is one of the world's most opulent hotels, known for its 7-star luxury and remarkable Arabian architecture. This grand building exemplifies Abu Dhabi's commitment to excellence in hospitality and design.

Another perspective of Emirates Palace

Architectural Splendor

This angle of the Emirates Palace showcases its impressive faÃ§ade and meticulous landscaping. The hotel spans over a kilometer from wing to wing and features 114 domes, with the central dome rising to 72 meters.

Interior view of Emirates Palace

Interior Elegance

The interior of Emirates Palace is adorned with gold, marble, and crystal elements that create an atmosphere of unparalleled luxury. The meticulous attention to detail is evident in every aspect of this magnificent hotel.

Grand hall of Emirates Palace

Palatial Design

The grand hall of Emirates Palace exemplifies its palatial design philosophy, combining traditional Arabian elements with contemporary luxury. The hotel has become an icon of Abu Dhabi and a symbol of the emirate's hospitality.

Gardens of Emirates Palace

Landscaped Beauty

The meticulously maintained gardens of Emirates Palace create a lush oasis in the desert environment. The landscape design incorporates fountains, palm trees, and colorful floral arrangements that complement the architectural grandeur.

Lunch during the ICIP 2024 conference

Conference Hospitality

The ICIP 2024 organizers provided excellent catering throughout the event. This lunch showcases the attention to detail in every aspect of the conference, ensuring attendees could network and share ideas in a comfortable setting.

Beautiful beach in Abu Dhabi

Coastal Beauty

Abu Dhabi is blessed with stunning coastlines and pristine beaches. Taking a break from the conference to enjoy the turquoise waters and sandy shores provided a perfect balance between academic pursuits and relaxation.

Abu Dhabi Aquarium exhibit

Marine Life

The Abu Dhabi Aquarium offers visitors an immersive experience into the underwater world. This exhibit showcases the rich marine biodiversity found in the waters surrounding the UAE and beyond.

Colorful fish at Abu Dhabi Aquarium

Underwater Wonders

The vibrant colors of tropical fish create a mesmerizing display at the aquarium. Educational exhibits help visitors understand marine ecosystems and the importance of ocean conservation.

Large display tank at Abu Dhabi Aquarium

Ocean Depths

The large display tanks at the Abu Dhabi Aquarium allow visitors to witness the complex interactions of various marine species. The facility combines entertainment with education, promoting awareness about marine conservation.

Ray exhibit at Abu Dhabi Aquarium

Graceful Movements

Rays glide elegantly through the water in this specialized exhibit. The Abu Dhabi Aquarium houses various species of these fascinating creatures, allowing visitors to observe their unique locomotion and behavior.

The magnificent Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque

Architectural Masterpiece

The Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque is one of the world's largest mosques and a masterpiece of modern Islamic architecture. Its pure white marble exterior, featuring 82 domes and over 1,000 columns, creates an awe-inspiring presence.

Interior view of Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque

Interior Splendor

The interior of the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque is adorned with intricate floral designs, gold accents, and the world's largest hand-knotted carpet. The mosque accommodates over 40,000 worshippers and welcomes visitors of all faiths.

Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque illuminated at night

Nocturnal Beauty

At night, the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque is illuminated by a unique lighting system that reflects the phases of the moon. The ethereal blue lighting creates a spiritual ambiance that enhances the mosque's architectural features in a magical way.
