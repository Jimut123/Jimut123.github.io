Jimut Bahan Pal, Dripta Mj

A novel Deep learning architecture is proposed which can segment images at multiple scales, leading to high dice coefficient in majority of challenging datasets. A novel attention module is proposed, loss at different scales are optimised and segmentation masks are added using Bayesian Optimization.
