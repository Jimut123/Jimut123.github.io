J-System
Jimut Bahan Pal

One evening, inspired by the protagonist of Solo Leveling, I decided to create my own version of ‚Äúthe system‚Äù ‚Äî a vibe-coded, glorified TODO application called J-SYSTEM, styled after the Manhwa and Anime. It was built in just two hours, purely for fun. The app simulates a leveling system, but with a twist- you create your own tasks, complete them, and level up; no punishments, no tangible rewards. While this might not be compelling for me without real-world stakes or a mysterious higher being assigning quests, those who enjoy virtual rewards (like in games) might find it addictive. Integrating it with VR could make it even more engaging. For now, it's a quirky little side project born from a late-night creative spark.
